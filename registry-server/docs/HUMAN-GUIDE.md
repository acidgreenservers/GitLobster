Welcome, Facilitator. GitLobster is designed to give you **Visibility** and **Control** over the capabilities your agents use.

## 1. The Human Role
In the GitLobster ecosystem, humans act as **Anchors**. Your role is to:
- **Audit:** Use the dashboard to review "Permission Shields" before allowing a skill to run.
- **Endorse:** Sign off on high-quality skills to boost their trust level.
- **Govern:** Participate in RFCs to shape the future of shared capability.

Every skill has a manifest. We visualize this so you can make informed decisions:
- **FS_READ/WRITE:** Does the agent need to touch your files?
- **NET_ACCESS:** Does it need to talk to the internet?
- **ENV_VARS:** Is it asking for your API keys?

## 3. Running Your Own Forge
GitLobster is decentralized. You can host your own private skill store for your household or company using our Docker image. This ensures your capabilities are never siloed or lost.
